Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Get Swole app ("Get Swole," "the app," "we," "us") handles your information. Get Swole is operated by Harjit Singh. By using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

The short version: Get Swole is built to keep your data on your own device. We do not run servers that store your workouts, and we do not collect or sell your personal information.

Information stored on your device

All of the data you create in Get Swole — including your workout logs, programs, exercises, recovery scores, weights, reps, and settings — is stored locally on your device using your device's built-in database. This information stays on your device. We do not have access to it, and it is not transmitted to us or to any third party by the app.

Device backups

Because your data is stored in the app's standard storage location on your device, it may be included in your device's own backups (for example, iCloud Backup or a computer backup) if you have those turned on. These backups are controlled entirely by you through your device and Apple account settings; they are not managed by Get Swole, and we cannot access them.

Notifications

Get Swole can send you local notifications — for example, to alert you when a rest timer finishes. These notifications are generated on your device. We do not send push notifications from a server, and using them does not transmit any personal data to us.

Subscriptions and purchases

Get Swole offers an optional paid subscription that unlocks premium features. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We use a third-party service, RevenueCat, to manage and verify subscription status. When you make or restore a purchase, RevenueCat processes a limited amount of information, such as an anonymous app-generated user identifier and your subscription status, in order to determine whether you have access to premium features. This information is used solely to manage your subscription. We do not receive your name, payment-card details, or Apple ID through this process — Apple handles all payment information directly. You can review RevenueCat's privacy practices at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy, and Apple's at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/.

Information we do not collect

Get Swole does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services. We do not track your activity across other apps or websites, we do not build advertising profiles, and we do not sell or share your personal information.

Children's privacy

Get Swole is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You are in control of your data. You can delete your workout data at any time from within the app, and uninstalling the app removes its locally stored data from your device. Managing or deleting your device backups is done through your device and Apple account settings.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.
